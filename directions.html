Santa Barbara Sailing Center, SB, CA 93109

As you turn into the Santa Barbara Harbor parking lot, make a left and pull up to the kiosk. Pickup a ticket and we'll validate it for you. Validated parking is $3.

From the North:

  • Take Hwy 101 South to Santa Barbara
  • Exit at Castillo (Harbor Exit)
  • Right on Castillo, get in Left lane immediately
  • Continue toward the ocean. The Road ends at Cabrillo/Shoreline intersection - 2nd right
  • Right on Cabrillo/Shoreline
  • Left at Harbor Way and Left again into the Harbor parking lot

From the South:

  • Take Hwy 101 North to Santa Barbara
  • Exit at Garden/Laguna (stay on the left for Garden St)
  • Left on Garden St, drive until road ends at the beach - 2nd light
  • Right on Cabrillo, drive to 3rd stoplight - Harbor Way
  • Left at Harbor Way and Left again into the Harbor parking lot

Parking Instructions:

  • Drive through the first lot and to the automatic gate (be careful of bike path travelers here).
  • You may park in either lot, but not in a yellow trailer stall.
  • The boat launch ramp is next to the SB Sailing Center / Mini Mart.
