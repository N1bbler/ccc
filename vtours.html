Observation Deck. See for yourself.

Join the crew of the Channel Cat while we spot whales, dolphins, and the giant Pelicans diving for their food. Relax in total comfort on the bow while watching the beautiful Santa Barbara beaches, along with the Santa Ynez mountain range. You'll see the Channel Islands of Anacapa, Santa Cruz, Santa Rosa, and San Miguel from the bow, or pilot house.

Salon. Room to relax.

Dine in total comfort while watching the ocean from your table. The ship has 10.5 foot ceilings, and 3,000 sq ft of walking space. We offer the facilities of a fine hotel with a spectacular coastal cruise. The ship offers a beautifully appointed Birdseye Maple commercial bar. We offer wine tastings, and serve some of the best local wines.

Aft-Deck. Lots of room to mingle.

This deck is a covered back deck with Teak tables and chairs for dining, and lots of room all around for watching the coastline.

Pilot House. Where the action happens.

Passengers may visit with the Captain in the pilot house, and observe the mechanics of sailing the 107 ton ship. Our hi-tech underwater equipment will amaze you.

Upper Deck. Take in the view of the Bay.

After visiting the Pilot House, enjoy amazing views from the Upper Deck from an elevated vantage point.

Extreme Yachts Show: Video Tour

Extreme Yachts travels the country to see how top designers and nautical engineers create some of the most amazing vessels ever to hit the high seas.

Watch and enjoy this clip where the Channel Cat was featured on the show, including extensive views of the vessel and interviews with the crew.
